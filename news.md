## Surge Watch

Long-context **sparse attention** is surging back after last week's portable-runtime detour. [Hierarchical Sparse Attention Done Right](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02980) is the week's clearest accelerator — 55→67 HF upvotes with GitHub stars doubling (41→83) in just three days (Jul 10→13), pitching "infinite context" as the target. It's riding the wake of [MiniMax Sparse Attention](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13392), which has settled into the category's loudest signal at 150 HF upvotes and ~370 stars — comfortably past the 100-upvote mark.

Two other fresh papers debuted strong rather than climbed: [BlockPilot](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31315) (diffusion-based speculative decoding) landed at 76 HF upvotes and [Grouped Query Experts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20945) at 79, both holding flat since arrival — worth watching whether either converts a loud launch into sustained traction.
