[DMax](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08302v1) is the new breakout: parallel decoding for diffusion LLMs jumped from 17 to 38 HF upvotes and 5 to 75 GitHub stars in two days. Only two data points, but that's the sharpest initial velocity we've seen since TriAttention launched.

[TriAttention](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04921v1) crossed the 100 HF upvote mark and sits at 433 GitHub stars — but the pace is cooling. Stars went from +99/day to ~+25/day over the last two readings. Still the biggest paper of the week, just entering cruise mode.

[MegaTrain](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05091v1) tells the opposite story: HF upvotes plateaued at 40, but GitHub stars *accelerated* — 190 to 313 (+123) in two days, faster than the prior +91 jump. Practitioners are cloning the repo even as the HF crowd moves on.

MARS has stalled at 29/15 after the initial spike — the MegaTrain-trajectory comparison from last time didn't hold up.
